Our Academic Programs

Waterstone College provides an enriching curriculum that focuses on academic rigor, creativity, and personal development. We aim to foster critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a global perspective in all our students.

Subjects Offered

  • Mathematics – Develop logical reasoning and problem-solving skills.
  • Science – Explore Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Environmental Science.
  • English – Strengthen communication and literary analysis skills.
  • Social Sciences – Learn about history, geography, and human behavior.
  • Languages – French, Spanish, and more.
