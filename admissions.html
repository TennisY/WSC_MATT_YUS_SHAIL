Admissions Process

We are thrilled that you are considering Waterstone College for your child’s education. Our admissions process is designed to ensure we welcome students who align with our values of excellence, integrity, and community.

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form.
  • Recent school report (if applicable).
  • Proof of identity (birth certificate/passport).
  • Parent/Guardian contact details.

How to Apply

To begin the application process, please complete the online application form on our website or visit our admissions office for assistance. Our admissions team will guide you every step of the way.

Important Dates

  • Application deadline: December 1st.
  • Interviews: Early January.
  • Decision letters sent: Late January.

We look forward to welcoming your family to our Waterstone College community!
