Our Heritage

Waterstone College has a proud history of providing outstanding education since its founding in 1994. Through its dedication to academic excellence and community development, the college has grown to become a beacon of learning.

Our school’s founding principles of integrity, excellence, and community continue to guide us as we expand our programs and reach new heights in education.
